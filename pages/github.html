TeteLearning

TeteLearning is an idea that I've been playing around with that would match up people wanting to be a mentor with people who are looking for a mentor. Initially it will just be a mentor registry but ultimately it would include the ability to assess someone's knowledge in a subject and promote them to mentor based on this.
